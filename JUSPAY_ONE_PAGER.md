### India's AI-Powered Talent Commerce Platform — Partnership Proposal

## What Is Influencia?

**Not just an influencer platform.** Influencia is India's **AI-powered talent commerce platform** — a single marketplace where brands discover, match, engage, and pay **any type of talent**: from nano-influencers to Bollywood celebrities, IPL cricketers, event performers, and corporate thought leaders.

**One platform. One AI engine. One payment rail (Juspay).**

---

## The Problem

₹13,000+ Crore flows through India's talent commerce market annually — influencer deals, celebrity endorsements, sports sponsorships, event bookings — through **WhatsApp forwards, manual bank transfers, and zero payment infrastructure**. Whether it's a ₹5,000 nano-influencer post or a ₹5 Crore celebrity endorsement, the same chaos exists: no discovery, no evaluation, no structured payments, no measurement.
